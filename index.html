台灣性感Taiwan Sensuality

這是一場關於感知台灣的展覽。性感，不是身體的裸露，而是感官的全然開放——當你踩在台灣的土地上，感受山的冷冽、海的溫潤、城市的喧囂與田野的靜默，那就是台灣的性感。

This exhibition explores the sensory experience of Taiwan — its warmth, its textures, its sounds and silences. Photography, typography, and interactive design come together to invite you into a visceral encounter with this island.

藝術家 王昱韋 / 8rian Wang
